🌱 Welcome to Garden Planner!

Here's how to plan your garden in 5 easy steps:

  • 📐
    Set your garden size Enter the width and length of your outdoor space in the top-left panel, then click Apply Size.
  • ➕
    Add beds, pots, or ground areas Use the sidebar buttons to add a raised bed, an in-ground planting area, or a pot. Drag them around the canvas to position them.
  • 🌿
    Assign plants Click a bed or pot to select it, then pick plants from the dropdown in the right panel. The planner will flag companion plants and conflicts automatically.
  • 🌱
    Arrange plants in each bed Click 🌱 Plant Organizer in the sidebar to open a square-foot grid for each bed. Drag emojis to rearrange, or paint cells using the palette below the grid.
  • 🛒
    Get your shopping list & build plan Click 🛒 Shopping List for a store-ready materials list, or 🪵 Bed Builder for lumber, soil volume, and cost estimates for each raised bed.
  • 💾
    Save your plan — don't lose your work! This app has no account or auto-save. Use 💾 Save Plan (JSON) in the sidebar to download your layout as a file, and 📂 Load Plan (JSON) to restore it later. Do this before closing the tab.
